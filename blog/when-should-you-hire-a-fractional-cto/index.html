When Should You Hire a Fractional CTO?

A fractional CTO provides strategic technology leadership without the cost of a full-time executive. But when is the right time to bring one on?

1. You’re building your first product

Founders often need help choosing the right architecture, stack, and roadmap.

2. Engineering velocity is slowing down

A fractional CTO can diagnose bottlenecks and improve execution.

3. You’re preparing for fundraising

Investors want clarity on technology, risks, and scalability.
